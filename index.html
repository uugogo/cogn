An open ecosystem for cognitive data analysis

CogniEco harnesses the power of internet by gathering, sorting and displaying real-time situational reports from weather issues such as flooding or traffic congestion. Our powerful toolset makes this information available in real-time to everyone in need of daily information, and meanwhile conducts predictive analysis to better inform prepare for future planning.

Collect

Real-time two-way communication using Twitter, to collect citizen infrastructure reports.

Analyse

Using spatial database technology, CogniEco can use real time data to develop predictive tools to ready cities for future disasters and become more resilient in day to day operations.

Communicate

Real time maps help citizens plan and react to changing infrastructural conditions, communicating through CogniEco to utilize the power of citizen knowledge and crowd sourced information to navigate fluxuating infrastructure.

CogniEco is open platform. It's a place, people can upload, share data files and chat with others.
